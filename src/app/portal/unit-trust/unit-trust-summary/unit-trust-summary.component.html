Life Wrapper

Life Wrapper Benefit: This is a benefit accessible to your beneficiaries in the unfortunate event of your passing on. It supports them in covering the resultant expenses of paying their last respects. The cover valued at Kshs 50,000 payable within 48 hours of filing a claim from and submission of an original ID/certified ID copy of the deceased and nominated beneficiary and a burial permit

Terms and conditions

  • Must hold at least one of the following unit trusts accounts: Equity fund, Money Market Fund, Balanced Fund, Toboa Investment Plan & Bond Fund
  • Must have a minimum bakance of KES 200,000.00 at the end of every month
  • Minimum age 18 years (age last birthday) and maximum age 74 years (age last birthday) of the account holder
  • Only eligible to individual clients and not corporate clients. For joint accounts, only one holder will be covered
  • Exclusions: Benefit shall not be payable if death is brought about or accelerated by: Suicide (whether sane or insane):
    • The influence of alcohol
    • Engaging in aviation except as a passenger on a recognized airline
    • Participants in any hazardous sport or pursuit including rock climbing, scuba diving, hang-gliding and aided speed contests of any kind
    • Committing a felony
    • An unsound mind
    • Any biological and chemical incidents
    • Use of illegal drugs
    • Nuclear and radiation disaster
  • Cession: You may NOT cede your Contract in any way
  • Only amendment allowd to the policy is change in the beneficiary

FUND DETAILS

Old Mutual Money Market Fund

The objective of the Old Mutual Money Market Fund is to generate total return through investing in a range of securities such as Government of Kenya Treasury Bills, fixed deposit, instruments and near casj in the Kenyan market while proc=viding the most conservative risk exposure to investors. Minimum investment amount is Kshs 1,000. This fund is suitable for an investor who would like a safe parking place for their funds. The client is advised to invest their money for at least a year

No initial fee is chargeable on the Money Market Fund

Old Mutual Equity Fund

The objective of the Old Mutual Money Market Fund is to generate long-term capital groath through active management investing principally. in a well diversified portfolio of equities i.e stocks and shares across several sectors of the Kenyan economy, in earnings of corporations and assets with a medium high risk profile. Minimum investing amount is Kshs 50,000. This fund is suitable for investors seeking uperior returns over the medium to long term and who have a tolerance for fluctuations in capital value. The recommended investment period is 4-7 years

Old Mutual Balanced Fund

The objective of the Old Balanced Fund is to ahcieve steady regular income from the money market portion of the fund and capital growth in the medium to long term from the equity portion while carryong only a medium level of risk. Minimum investment amounr is Kshs 50,000. This fund is suitable for the medium risk investor who is looking for a mixture of safety, income and capital appreciation. The recommended investment period is 3-5 years

Toboa Investment Plan

The Toboa investmetn plan is a facility that allows investors to make monthly contributions / investments of Kshs 1,000 into the Balanced Fund. The fund is suitable for the medium risk investor who is looking for a mixture of safety, income and capital appreciation. The recommended investment period is 3-5 years

Old Mutual Bond Fund

The objective of the Old Mutual Bond Fund is to achieve a reasonable level of current income and steady growth of capital by investing primarily in a well-diversified portfolio of fixed income securities issued by the Kenyan government and corporations with some offshore element while carrying a low - medium level of risk. Minimum investment amount is Kshs 50,000. This fun is suitable for a low to medium risk investor who is seeking a reasonable level of current income or a steady growth of capital from the reinvestment of the distribution income

Fund Charges

A service fee of 1% per annum + VAT os applicable to the Money Market Fund, a service fee of 3.37% per annum + VAT is applicable to Equity, Balance and Toboa Funds and a service fee of 3% per annum + VAT is applicable to the Bond Fund
